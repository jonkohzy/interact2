FAQs

  • Has registration closed?

    Registration has closed! A big thank you to over 150 participants from 11 schools for signing up, from all of us in the Organising Team. We will get in touch with participants shortly via email or Whatsapp.

  • Who is the target audience?

    Any student who's in Secondary 3 to Junior College 1, who's interested in serving the community! Preferably, participants should also be involved in student leadership, or have some volunteering and service learning experiences.

  • Are teachers allowed to attend our programmes?

    Teachers may contact us via email, servicelearningsummit@gmail.com , to express their interest in observing our programmes.

  • Will there be a registration fee?

    Nope! All programmes will take place online. In addition, Hwa Chong Interact strongly believes in the value of accessibility and inclusivity, and will strive to keep our initiatives open to all participants.

  • What if I'm not able to make it for some parts of the Summit?

    Commitment to our program is important as external speakers have taken their time off to be involved with us. As such, we hope that all participants can commit to the full duration of the Summit.
